What We Have Done

Nationwide Campaign International Collaboration Scholarship Program

Initiatives

Conducting educational workshops in schools and communities to promote awareness and understanding of the Philippine Eagle and its critical role in the ecosystem.

Collaborating with local authorities and wildlife conservation organizations to develop and implement eagle protection programs.

Involvements

Actively participating in regional and national conferences on wildlife conservation to share insights and gain support.

Engaging in lobbying efforts to strengthen wildlife protection laws and regulations.

Activities and Participations

Organizing annual "Eagle Awareness Week" events to engage communities in conservation activities.

Participating in habitat restoration projects to ensure the survival of the Philippine Eagle and its ecosystem.

Accomplishments

Successfully petitioned for increased penalties for illegal hunting and trading of Philippine Eagles.

Secured funding for the construction of a rehabilitation center for injured and rescued eagles.

Led community clean-up initiatives, removing tons of trash from eagle habitats.

Facilitated the planting of 10,000 trees in critical eagle habitats to promote a sustainable environment.
