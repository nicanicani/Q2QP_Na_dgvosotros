About WOH

We are the Wings of Hope (WOH), a passionate community of young advocates dedicated to raising awareness about the extinction crisis facing the Philippine Eagle. Our organization was founded in 2018 by a group of environmentally conscious young individuals who recognized the urgent need to protect and preserve the majestic Philippine Eagle, one of the most endangered species in the world.

What we have done

Successfully organized nationwide awareness campaigns, reaching thousands of people through social media and community events.

Partnered with international organizations to raise awareness globally about the plight of the Philippine Eagle.

Established a scholarship program to support students pursuing degrees in environmental science and wildlife conservation.
