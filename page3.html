Who We Are

"Wings of Hope" is a dynamic group of young individuals united by our love for nature and commitment to saving the Philippine Eagle from extinction. Our diverse team includes environmentalists, educators, and wildlife enthusiasts working together to make a difference.

Our Values

Our vision is to create a future where the Philippine Eagle thrives in a protected and sustainable environment, ensuring the well-being of both the species and its habitat.

Our mission is to raise awareness, inspire action, and actively participate in initiatives that contribute to the conservation of the Philippine Eagle and its ecosystems.

Join us!

Interested in joining our cause? There are various ways you can contribute:


    a. Donate: Support our initiatives financially to fund awareness campaigns and conservation projects.
    b. Volunteer: Become an active member of our team, participating in events, workshops, and advocacy programs.
    c. Spread the Word: Share our message on social media and encourage others to join the movement.
    d. Educate: Teach others about the importance of preserving the Philippine Eagle and its habitat.
